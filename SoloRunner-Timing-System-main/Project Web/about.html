About Us

" Welcome to SoloRunner! We are a team of passionate and innovative individuals from the University of Moratuwa's Faculty of Information Technology. Our goal is to revolutionize the way athletes train on running tracks by providing state-of-the-art timing systems. With our cutting-edge technology, we aim to empower athletes to train autonomously, track their progress, and optimize their performance effectively.

By ensuring accurate timing, immediate feedback, and comprehensive data analysis, we are dedicated to enhancing the training experiences of runners of all levels. Join us on our journey to make running training more accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for everyone! "
